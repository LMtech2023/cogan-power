No one can prepare for the moment an accident strikes. Although accidents happen, some incidents cause substantial injuries and losses — inflicting great pain and suffering, resulting in job loss, high medical bills, loss of future earnings, and much more.

Whether you are a victim of medical malpractice, a railroad accident, a motor vehicle collision, or another catastrophe, you can count on Cogan & Power, P.C. to stand up for what you deserve. Through tireless negotiation and relentless focus, we help clients achieve favorable settlements — and we are well-qualified to handle even the most complex cases.
